Sugar Features

Sugar Calculator Features

Precision Sugar Calculations

Accurate sugar measurements based on ingredient weight, with real-time conversion between grams, teaspoons, and cups. Perfect for baking and cooking.

Health Monitoring

Automatic health recommendations based on WHO guidelines for daily sugar intake (25g added sugar/day). Get warnings when exceeding recommended limits.

Unit Conversion

Seamless switching between metric (g/kg) and imperial (oz/lb) measurement systems. Includes baking-specific units like teaspoons and cups.

Natural Sweetener Equivalents

Convert sugar amounts to healthier alternatives like stevia, monk fruit, honey, and syrup. Includes precise conversion ratios for each sweetener.

Customizable Ratios

Adjust sugar percentage from 1% to 25% for different recipes, preservation needs, or dietary requirements. Ideal for desserts, beverages, and preserves.

Recipe Scaling

Easily scale recipes up or down with the built-in recipe scaling feature. Adjust sugar amounts proportionally for any serving size.
