At Wan Records, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Salt and Sugar Calculator applications.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) through our applications. Our calculators operate entirely on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to use or share. All calculations and data remain on your device.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies on our applications.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties. No data is collected that could be disclosed.

Children's Information

Our applications are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
